Hello, I'm Thomas. I am an Algorithms Research Scientist at Facebook. Previously I was the Chief of Machine Learning at the Natural Language Processing startup, SupWiz, and a Postdoctoral researcher in Theoretical Computer Science at the Basic Algorithms Research group (BARC) in Copenhagen. I did my PhD with Rasmus Pagh on the Scalable Similarity Search project, and previously I have been at University of Texas at Austin, and University of Oxford.

My research has primarily involved the theoretical foundations of machine learning and massive data. This includes similarity search, high dimensional geometry, sketching and derandomization.
